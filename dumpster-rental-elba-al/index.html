Frequently Asked Questions

1. What sizes of dumpsters do you offer?

We maintain a full range of roll-off and front-load container sizes—from small 10-yard units up to 40-yard models—to suit everything from minor home clean-ups to major construction projects. How long can I keep the dumpster on site? Typically you rent a container for a standard period (e.g., 7-14 days), but extensions are often possible. We will quote you a rental period and you can request additional time if necessary.

2. Are there items that cannot go into the dumpster?

Yes. We provide a clear list of prohibited materials (such as certain hazardous chemicals, asbestos, motor oil, and large quantities of liquid waste) to stay compliant with local disposal centers.

3. How much does it cost?

Pricing depends on container size, rental period, load weight, type of waste and access logistics. We provide transparent quotes and alert you to possible extra charges (like overweight or inaccessible placement) upfront.

4. Do you offer recycling or diversion services?

Yes. We partner with local recycling centres and will help you separate and divert eligible materials. We provide guidance on what can be recycled and include documentation where applicable.

Elba, AL Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

Elba is a city in and the county seat of Coffee County, Alabama, United States. It is the official seat, although there are two county courthouses, with the other one being located in the town of Enterprise. At the time of the 2010 U.S. census, Elba's population was 3,940. Elba is part of the Enterprise micropolitan statistical area.
